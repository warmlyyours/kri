New Hampton Kitchen Floor Plan with Island

Installation Options:

TempZone™ 3.7-Watt Floor Heating Cable:

“The WarmlyYours TempZone™ 3.7-Watt Floor Heating Cable allows you to bring radiant warmth to any room. TempZone™ Floor Heating Cable (Twin) is ultra-low EMF by design. It features a twin conductor with a 15’ cold lead and is available in two voltage options and a range of lengths to meet the requirements of any installation. In 120V, we offer lengths ranging from 30 to 432 linear feet . For 240V, our lengths range from 55 to 864 linear feet.” 

Stone Decor Fog 11 in. x 12 in. x 10 mm Marble Mosaic Floor and Wall Tile:

“Enhance any room with the high-end look of marble coupled with the eye catching herringbone pattern. This marble floor and wall mosaic can be mounted as a whole or cut into strips to be used as an accent on a wall or floor. Great for areas of the home that will be wet such as baths and showers as well.”

Rosborough Display Stand:

“Encourage a rustic atmosphere with the Berlin Cabinet from Dutchbone. Featuring recycled wood with glass, two doors that have a magnetic closure, and three fixed shelves to organize or simply use for decorative items.”
